The session

What happens in 1h30
The approach
No lecture.Just tasting.

We don't start with a map on a screen. We start with a glass. Theory follows sensation, always. You'll understand the wines better because you've already tasted them.

01
Welcome & context

A brief intro to what "natural wine" actually means, and doesn't mean. Why these producers, why the Mediterranean, why now. Five minutes, no slides.

02
Three wines, three conversations

Each wine opens a different chapter: a terroir, a grape variety, a production choice. We taste slowly, compare, disagree. There's no right answer, just more precise observations.

03
The story behind the bottle

For each wine: who made it, where the vines are, what the season was like. Context that changes how you taste, and gives you something to say when you serve it at home.

04
Where to find these wines

A list of every wine tasted, with producers, importers, and a few addresses in Marseille and online. You leave with something concrete.
